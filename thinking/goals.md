# Storyloop Goals

These goals guide the ongoing development of Storyloop as a creator-focused analytics companion. They describe how the product should support creators day-to-day.

1. **Grow the channel** – Provide insights and automations that help creators identify what resonates, repeat successes, and expand their audience consistently.
2. **Journal and keep track of video projects and ideas** – Maintain a unified timeline that captures journal entries, experiments, and concepts alongside performance data.
3. **Take a project to completion with high quality and help** – Offer structured workflows and feedback loops so creators can plan, execute, and polish each release with confidence.
4. **Brainstorm ideas, expand existing ones, and get feedback** – Enable ideation support, collaborative refinement, and AI-assisted critique to evolve concepts into compelling stories.
5. **Keep track of progress and improvements** – Surface clear progress indicators and retrospectives so creators can see how their craft evolves over time.

These objectives reinforce Storyloop’s mission to be a creator’s analytics journal and growth coach, pairing narrative insight with actionable metrics.
